2. About Korea

South Korea is located in Eastern Asia and sits on the southern half of the Korean peninsula. The only land borders are with North Korea with which South Korea shares the Korean peninsula. The Korean peninsula is surrounded by the Yellow Sea, the South Sea and the East Sea.

South Korea is an extraordinary country filled with beautiful beaches, thriving cities, ancient temples, remarkable natural scenery and most importantly, friendly people. South Korea is home to an endless number of world class attractions and annual festivals that foreigners and locals alike enjoy each year.
